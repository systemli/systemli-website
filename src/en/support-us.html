Everyone who works for systemli.org does this as an unpaid volunteer. Besides time, providing our services and infrastructure continuously costs a lot of money. The news about PRISM, Tempora and the new spying initiative of the BND (German intelligence service) show how important an independent infrastructure based on solidarity is.

There are several possibilities to support us:

Enhance our wiki

We provide several how-tos and handy tips in our wiki. Enhancements, updates and translations are more than welcome. If something is missing, go ahead: Create an account and get involved.

Questions, criticism, enhancements

To provide a better service, we need your feedback. Is something not working as supposed? Is something missing? In such cases, we encourage you to write us an e-mail!

Improving our software projects and webpage

Some of our Ansible roles and other software projects can be found on our Github page. We're always happy about patches and improvements. Moreover, we want to raise your attentention for the source code of our website. Here you can add new content or translate our website into other languages. We would be pleased, if we could reach more people with comprehensible content.

Shirts and merchandise

You can get our shirts and merchandise (stickers, bookmarks, postcards) in the web shop of Black Mosquito.

Donations

To be able to provide this infrastructure in the future, we are dependent on your donations. We would be very happy if every user could donate a little bit of money to us or even arrange a standing order. Every donation helps.

We would love for every project that uses our services to donate 100 Euros annually. But this is no criteria for exclusion. For projects without money we will find other solutions.

For individuals who use our services it would be appropriate to donate 1 Euro per account per month.
